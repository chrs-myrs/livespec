# Regulatory Compliance

## Requirements
- [!] System complies with all federal and state lending regulations.
  - Interest calculations comply with Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
  - Late fees comply with state-specific maximum limits
  - Payment schedules disclose total interest paid over loan term
  - Annual Percentage Rate (APR) calculated per regulation
  - All required disclosures provided to borrowers
  - Fair lending practices enforced (no discriminatory patterns)

## Validation
- Legal review confirms TILA compliance
- State-by-state late fee limits correctly configured
- Disclosure documents include all required information
- APR calculations match federal formula
- Fair lending audit finds no discriminatory patterns
- Regulatory filing accepted without corrections
